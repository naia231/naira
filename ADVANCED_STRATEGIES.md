## 1. The "AI Grid" Strategy (DePIN Inference)
Instead of mining crypto, you sell your GPU compute to AI networks for task-based rewards.
- **Project Examples**: Heurist, Ritual, Aethir, Morpheus.
- **How it works**: You run a Python worker that generates AI images (Stable Diffusion) or text (LLMs). Each "job" pays you in tokens.
- **Profit Potential**: **$2.00 - $5.00 / day per Tesla T4**. 
- **Scale (40 accounts)**: **$80 - $200 / day**.
- **Pros**: 100x more profitable than mining. No Docker required (Python-native).
- **Cons**: High GPU usage. Requires "The Residential Bridge" (see below) to bypass datacenter bans.

## 2. The "Swarm Airdrop Farmer"
Automated daily interactions on 40+ independent accounts to qualify for high-value token airdrops.
- **Project Examples**: Berachain, Linea, Monad, Aleo.
- **How it works**: Use **Playwright** (Python browser automation) to perform daily "swaps" and "claims" on testnets.
- **Profit Potential**: **$10,000 - $100,000 per year** (Variable but huge).
- **Pros**: Extremely low resource usage. Very low detection risk.
- **Cons**: Not "instant" daily cash; requires patience for the airdrop event.

## 3. The "Residential Bridge" (The Secret Sauce)
Google and Kaggle IPs are labeled as "Datacenter." Many high-paying apps (Grass, UpRock, AI networks) ban these IPs.
- **The Fix**: You buy a **Residential Proxy** pool. You configure your Python script to route all traffic through these proxies.
- **The Illusion**: To the network, your Tesla T4 looks like it is sitting in a house in London or New York.
- **Cost**: ~$50/month for a pool.
- **Gain**: Unlocks rewards that are otherwise blocked, increasing earnings by 500%.
